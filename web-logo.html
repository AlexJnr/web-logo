I designed this logo according to the requirements specified in the textbook.
First of all, the logo is unique to my project and represents it well. It is scalable, because it does not lose any details even at small sizes and can be read easily. In addition, it does not contain any images, which is why its quality does not suffer at larger sizes. Consisting of just two words of different colors, the logo is simple and does not exceed the recommended limit of the number of colors. It is reproducible in black only and can be easily reversed out. Lastly, it is almost of a square shape, which makes it convenient to use in any layouts.
